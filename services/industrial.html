Our Industrial Services

OGC INDUSTRIES brings deep expertise in industrial electrical systems, from design through installation, testing, and ongoing maintenance. We understand the critical nature of electrical systems in manufacturing and industrial operations.

Services We Offer

High-Voltage Systems

Design and installation of 208V through 480V+ distribution systems. Includes transformer installation, switchgear, and proper grounding for industrial operations.

Motor Controls & Drives

Installation of motor starters, variable frequency drives (VFDs), and control systems. Includes programming and optimization for efficiency.

Equipment Installation

Electrical connections for specialized manufacturing and industrial equipment including machinery, pumps, compressors, and process controls.

Power Distribution

Complete electrical distribution systems including panels, breakers, disconnects, and proper sizing for industrial loads.

Emergency Power Systems

Generator installation and backup power systems with automatic transfer switches to maintain critical operations during outages.

Grounding & Lightning Protection

Comprehensive grounding systems and lightning protection to safeguard equipment and personnel from electrical hazards.

Process Control Systems

Specialized electrical systems for process control, heating, cooling, and specialized industrial processes.

Industrial Maintenance

Preventive maintenance programs and emergency repair services to minimize downtime and maintain operational efficiency.
