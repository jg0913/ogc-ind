Our Story

OGC INDUSTRIES was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional electrical contracting services with integrity, safety, and excellence at the core of everything we do. From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted partner for industrial, commercial, and residential clients, we've maintained our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to serve diverse industries and communities, helping power businesses and homes across the region.

Our Mission

To deliver professional, reliable, and innovative electrical solutions that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and integrity.
